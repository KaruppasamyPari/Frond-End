Rohit Sharma

Rohit Sharma is an Indian cricketer and the current captain of the Indian national team in limited-overs formats. He is known for his elegant batting style and has set numerous records in international cricket. He plays for the Mumbai Indians in the Indian Premier League (IPL) and has led them to multiple titles. Rohit is celebrated for his ability to score big hundreds and is one of the few players to have achieved the double century in ODIs. He is also known for his aggressive batting style and leadership qualities, making him one of the most successful captains in IPL history
