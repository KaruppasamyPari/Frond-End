Welcome to the World of Richard Mille


Discover the luxury and innovation of Richard Mille watches. Explore our history, iconic models, and the latest releases for 2025.

Richard Mille (RM) watches are renowned high-end luxury timepieces, distinguished by their pioneering innovation, technical mastery, and iconic designs. The brand, established in 2001 by Dominique Guenat and Richard Mille in Les Breuleux, Switzerland, has become a benchmark in luxury horology. The RM 001 Tourbillon, introduced in 2001, marked the brand's debut, featuring an ergonomic tonneau-shaped case, torque screws, a tourbillon calibre, power reserve indicator, and torque indicator. RM watches are known for their use of advanced and often unconventional materials, such as titanium, carbon TPT, ceramic, and even sapphire, borrowed from sectors like Formula 1 racing and aeronautics, contributing to their exceptional lightness, durability, and unique aesthetics.
